Pampatar Volleyball Beach Club

Our beach volleyball club was founded in Pampatar, Margarita Island on 17 March 2005. We offer a fun and supportive environment for players of all levels, with programs and events designed to build lasting friendships and improve skills. As a member, you'll enjoy exclusive discounts and perks, such as free coaching sessions and discounted tournament fees. Join us today and experience the thrill of playing beach volleyball with like-minded individuals while enjoying the benefits of our supportive community.
